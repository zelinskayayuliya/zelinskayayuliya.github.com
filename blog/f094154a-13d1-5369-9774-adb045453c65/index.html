GemlucArt - 2019

I am honored to be present at the contemporary art competition at GemlucArt Monaco. I am thrilled that my artworks have been given the opportunity to be exhibited here.

It is with great pleasure that I donate one of my paintings to the Contest of Contemporary Art named GemlucArt placed under the honorary presidency of Her Royal Highness the Princess of Hanover. Nearly ten million people worldwide died of cancer in 2018. If contributing one of my paintings will help fund research to eradicate this dreaded killer disease and save lives, then my Art will have served humanity in the most meaningful way.

I especially want to thank Laurence Garbatini for everything that was done by her on this noble goal.
