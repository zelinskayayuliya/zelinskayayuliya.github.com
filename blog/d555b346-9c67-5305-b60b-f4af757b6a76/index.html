Venice

I am honored to have received a special award at the International Biennial of Nations in The Court of Misericordia in Venice, Italy. I am thrilled that my artworks will be on exhibition at the Diocesan Museum Francesco Gonzaga - Mantua. I want to thank all of those who chose me for this very special honor. I especially want to thank Mr. Salvatore Russo, Mr. Francesco Saverio Russo and Mr. Marco Rebuzz for everything that was done by them on my behalf.
