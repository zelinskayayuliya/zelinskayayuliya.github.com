Art Without Borders: Modern Leaders Through the Eyes of Artists

I am going to carry out an International Call for Artists, "Art Without Borders: Modern Leaders Through the Eyes of Artists," during which displays of modern presidents and other acting leaders will be exhibited. The purpose of this promotion is to help artists establish contacts with each other and to hold joint exhibitions in different countries.

- If you are an artist and you have a portrait of a leader, please write to Yuliya Zelinskaya at: yuliya@zelinskaya-yuliya.com.
The portraits judged to b the best will appear in the publication.

- If you are a gallery owner and you are interested in future exhibitions on the subject, "Modern Leaders through the Eyes of Artists," please write to Yuliya Zelinskaya at:
zelinskaya.yuliya.art@gmail.com

The promotion is valid until July 1
